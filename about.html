About Me

My name is Javier. I am an Industrial Maintenance Engineer from Mexico, Graduated from UTM. I like to program and I am a fan of Linux and in the near future I'll try BSD.

I have been working in the automotive industry for like 12 years, but I have been specialized in automation sector like programming PLC and robots. Some of the programming software's that I know are: Studio 5000, CX-Programmer, RoboGuide. Also I know how to use some CAD software like: SolidWorks, AUTOCAD. Now I'm currently learning some programming languages like: Python, C, elisp.

I am a recent fan of Linux, I currently use "void linux", and as I wrote before; I hope to have a computer with "freebsd". I think that if it can be automated it should be done. In my maintenance career I had several jobs, most in the automotive industry, my knowledge and skills have taken me to jobs in different states of the country, most focused on automation.

I am currently working at AUTOLIV in H. Matamoros, Tamaulipas, and I am open to work in others companies and projects.
